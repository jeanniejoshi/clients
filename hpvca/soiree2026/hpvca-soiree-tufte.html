The Evening

An Evening of Purpose & Celebration. Join fellow advocates, survivors, and supporters for an unforgettable night overlooking the Hudson River.

Wine & Waterfront — Enjoy premium wines and craft cocktails with stunning Manhattan skyline views at City Vineyard's riverfront terrace.

Inspiring Voices — Hear from survivors, researchers, and advocates who are leading the fight against HPV-related cancers.

Live Auction — Bid on exclusive experiences and luxury items—all proceeds support education, prevention, and patient services.

Nestled on the Hudson River in Tribeca, City Vineyard offers breathtaking waterfront views and an intimate urban winery atmosphere—the perfect backdrop for an evening of purpose and celebration.
Your Impact — Your Ticket Changes Lives

Education — Fund evidence-based resources that help millions understand HPV prevention and screening.

Screening Access — Support programs that bring HPV screening to underserved communities across the country.

Patient Support — Ensure no one navigates an HPV diagnosis alone through our support services network.
