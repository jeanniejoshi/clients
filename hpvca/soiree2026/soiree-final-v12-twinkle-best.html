5% of all cancers worldwide are caused by HPV. Almost all of them are preventable.

This October, join us for an elegant evening of celebration, education, and fundraising. Together, we're building a future where no one faces HPV alone.

An Evening of Purpose & Celebration.

Wine & WaterfrontEnjoy premium wines and craft cocktails with stunning Manhattan skyline views at City Vineyard's riverfront terrace.
Inspiring VoicesHear from survivors, researchers, and advocates who are leading the fight against HPV-related cancers.
Live AuctionBid on exclusive experiences and luxury items—all proceeds support education, prevention, and patient services.

Nestled on the Hudson River in Tribeca, City Vineyard offers breathtaking waterfront views and an intimate urban winery atmosphere—the perfect backdrop for an evening of purpose and celebration.
